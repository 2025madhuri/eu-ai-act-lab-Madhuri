

\#\# Part 2: Client Briefs for Partner

\#\#\# Case 1

A private school chain wants to install AI-powered classroom cameras that monitor students during online and physical classes. The system analyzes facial expressions, eye movement, and voice tone to measure attention, emotional state, and learning engagement. Teachers receive weekly behavioral scores and alerts for low-performing students. Parents are informed through the school portal, but students cannot disable the monitoring system.

\---

\#\#\# Case 2

A health insurance company wants to implement an AI platform that evaluates customer lifestyle data, smartwatch activity, previous claims, and medical history to estimate future health risk levels. The AI system recommends premium adjustments and flags high-risk customers for manual review by insurance officers before policy approval.

\---

\#\#\# Case 3

An e-commerce company wants to launch an AI shopping assistant on its website that recommends products, answers customer questions, and generates personalized fashion suggestions using a third-party LLM. Customers interact directly with the assistant, but all final purchases remain fully controlled by users.

\---

\#\#\# Case 4

A restaurant chain wants to use AI to predict daily ingredient demand based on weather forecasts, local events, and historical sales data. The system generates kitchen preparation estimates to reduce food waste and improve inventory planning. Restaurant managers can manually override all recommendations.
